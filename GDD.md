# Sword & Magic — Game Design Document

**Platform:** Web browser (single HTML file), network multiplayer via Firebase  
**Visual style:** Pixel art (prototype: canvas shapes)  
**Session length:** ~20 minutes  
**Players:** 2 (simultaneous turns)

---

## Design Philosophy

- Simple rules, emergent complexity
- Complexity lives in decisions before resolution, not in resolution itself
- Information asymmetry creates mastery curve
- Every fight is a resource economy decision, not just a power check
- The game should never leave the "opening phase" feeling — always discovery, always tension
- Inspired by the best 20 minutes of HoMM and Civ: scouting, discovery, legendary run fantasy

---

## Win Condition

Reduce the enemy kingdom to 0 health.  
Each kingdom starts at **10 health**. Each lost War Party defense = **-1 health**.  
Map combat (POI capture) never affects kingdom health.

---

## Map

- Top-down **hexagonal grid** (pointy-top axial coordinates)
- Shape: **Star** — 6 arms extending from a radius-2 center cluster
  - Each arm is 3 hexes long (hex distances 3, 4, 5 from center)
  - Player 1 starts at **east arm end** (5, 0)
  - Player 2 (AI) starts at **west arm end** (−5, 0)
  - NE, SW, NW, SE arms contain POIs only
- **Fog of war** over unexplored areas; army reveals adjacent tiles on move
- **Road** connects both cities through the east arm, center, and west arm — revealed at game start, drawn as a visible dirt path
- Both cities and the road are visible from turn 1
- **POIs (Points of Interest)** are guarded by neutral stacks; strength shown as estimated range `~N`
- POIs on the road: (3, 0) near player, (−3, 0) near AI
- Center (0, 0) is highest-value POI — most contested
- Army can move **3 hexes per turn**, BFS-based (uniform terrain cost)

---

## Turns

- **Simultaneous** — all players act during the same turn window
- Turn advances only after **all players end their turn**
- UI: "End Turn" → button becomes "Waiting for opponent..." (disabled)
- Prototype: AI opponent simulated with a 1.5s delay
- Firebase multiplayer: waiting phase will gate on opponent's signal

---

## Resources

### Gold
- Base income: **+5 per turn**
- Spent on: drawing cards (1g per card — TBD), sending War Party (1g), buildings
- Generated by: POIs, Marketplace building, Transmute spell

### Mana
- Unlocked by: School building
- Base income: +2/turn from School
- Spent on: casting spells

### Glory Coins
- Earned by: winning an attack or defense against enemy kingdom (1 per win)
- Also dropped by: strong POIs
- Spent on:
  - Kingdom-wide +1 ATK: cost = 1 + existing ATK upgrades
  - Kingdom-wide +1 DEF: cost = 1 + existing DEF upgrades
  - Improving a unit type's unique ability
- Glory Coin upgrade costs are **independent** from Training Ground upgrade costs

---

## Army & Cards

- Units are cards with ATK, DEF, and a unique ability
- Card order matters — units proc left to right within each phase
- **Townhall** draws 3 random cards from the player's unit pool each turn
- Cards accumulate in hand; played by mustering into armies (TBD)

---

## Combat System

### Map Combat (POI Capture)
- Army total ATK must exceed stack DEF to capture
- All units that participated **die next turn**
- POI always drops **at least 1 unit** as reward
- If Meteor reduces a stack's DEF to ≤ 0, the stack dies without combat
- **Does NOT affect kingdom health**

### War Party (Kingdom Attack)
- Player spends **1 gold** to send a War Party to the enemy kingdom
- Defender sets up defense for **free** (no cost)
- Army moves along the road to the enemy city

#### Combat Phases
1. **Buff Phase** — passive auras activate (both sides simultaneously)
2. **Pre-Battle Phase** — determined by Speed
   - Attacker base Speed: 6 | Defender base Speed: 5 → attacker goes first by default
   - Slow debuff: −2 Speed to target army
   - If attacker Speed ≤ defender Speed → defender pre-battles first
   - Pre-battle actions: Archer fires (−1 DEF per archer), Assassin removes random enemy unit with DEF < 3
3. **Combat** — compare total ATK vs total DEF; higher wins
   - Attacker wins → defender loses 1 kingdom health
   - Attacker loses → defending army revealed and disappears next turn

#### Asymmetry
- Attacking costs 1 gold; defending is free but reveals and expires next turn
- Neither side is strictly better — intentional design

#### Flat Damage Rule
- Every lost fight = exactly **1 health**, regardless of army size
- Health tracks decision quality, not power

## Units (Tier 1)

| Unit | ATK | DEF | Ability |
|---|---|---|---|
| Warrior | 2 | 2 | None — baseline unit |
| Archer | 2 | 1 | Pre-Battle: −1 DEF to enemy side (stackable) |
| Assassin | 3 | 0 | Pre-Battle: removes random enemy unit with DEF < 3 |
| Apprentice | 2 | 1 | Combat: 10% chance per Apprentice to proc one random effect (Burn/Slow/Blessing/Protection/Resurrection) — unlocked by School |

**Deferred to later tiers:** Shieldbearer, Knight

---

## Buildings (Tier 1, cost: 4 gold each)

| Building | Effect |
|---|---|
| Townhall | Default. Draws 3 random cards/turn. Upgradeable (more spawns, guaranteed units) |
| Barracks | Adds Warrior to unit pool |
| Archery Range | Adds Archer to unit pool |
| Thieves Guild | Adds Assassin to unit pool |
| Marketplace | +2 gold income/turn |
| Training Ground | Spend gold for permanent +1 ATK or +1 DEF on a unit type. Cost: 1g + existing upgrades on that type |
| School | Unlocks Apprentice. Unlocks Mana (+2/turn). Unlocks 3 random Tier 1 spells |

Tier 2 and Tier 3 buildings: **TBD**

## Spells (Tier 1 only for now)

Spells have a tier and level; upgradeable.

### Spell Schools

| School | Theme |
|---|---|
| Reality | Resource conversion, mimicry, teleportation |
| Destruction | Damage/kill units and POIs on the world map |
| Light | Protect, resurrect, buff |
| Astral | Information and map awareness |
| Forbidden | Anti-magic mirror of all other schools (50% mana surcharge) |

### Tier 1 Spells

| Spell | School | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Transmute | Reality | 4 mana | → 1 gold. Upgradeable: +1 gold/level, max 4 |
| Meteor | Destruction | 4 mana | Target hex: −5 DEF on stack. Upgradeable: +5/level. Stack dies if DEF ≤ 0 |
| Vision | Astral | 2 mana | Clear fog of war in an area |
| Analyze | Astral | 1 mana | Reveal exact unit count of a target stack |

---

## Prototype Success Criteria

1. Player can draw cards, spend gold, build Tier 1 buildings
2. Combat phases resolve correctly: Buff → Pre-Battle → Combat
3. AI sends War Parties at player castle
4. POI capture works with the sacrifice mechanic
5. Health tracks to 0, game ends
6. Network multiplayer via Firebase (two players, shared URL)
